Breakfast Buns

Start your morning with a burst of flavor by enjoying these hearty Breakfast Buns filled with savory ham, fluffy scrambled eggs, and melty cheeseâ€”all seasoned with aromatic Italian herbs for an extra layer of deliciousness. Wrapped in soft, golden-baked buns, these handheld breakfast bites are perfect for busy mornings, brunch tables, or make-ahead meal prep. Easy to bake and endlessly customizable, they deliver warmth, comfort, and a hint of herby goodness in every bite!

Prep Time

10 mins

Cook Time

25 mins

Total Time

35 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 100g Cottage cheese
  • 150g Parmesan cheese
  • 8 slices Ham
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt & Pepper (to taste)
  • Mini burger buns
  • Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Whisk it up: In a measuring bowl, whisk together the eggs, cottage cheese, turkey ham, parmesan, and seasonings.
  2. Bake the eggs: Pour the mixture into the pan and bake for 15 minutes, or until set.
  3. Cool completely.
  4. Cut the eggs: With a round cutter, cut circles out of the pan of eggs.
  5. Assemble: Cut the eggs into rounds, sandwich them with cheese between muffins, and wrap them up.
