Lemon Mushroom Pasta

Lemon Mushroom Pasta is a light, creamy, and flavorful dish that brings together the earthy taste of mushrooms with the zesty freshness of lemon. This pasta features tender sautéed mushrooms, garlic, spinach, and a velvety cheese sauce, all tossed with al dente pasta and brightened with a splash of lemon juice and zest. It's the perfect blend of comfort and freshness.

Prep Time

15 mins

Cook Time

10 mins

Total Time

25 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 14 pcs mushrooms
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 250g spinach
  • 1/4 of a lemon juice
  • 1.5 cups heavy cream (+ 0.5 cup more if extra saucy)
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tsp garlic & herb seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 2-3 tbsp butter
  • 1 pack pasta (400-500g)
  • 1/4 cup pasta water
  • 1/2 cup pecorino romano
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. Slice mushrooms into even-sized pieces.
  3. Cook pasta until al dente and drain, reserving pasta water.
  4. Sauté mushrooms in coconut oil until golden brown (8-10 min).
  5. Add minced garlic and sauté 1 more minute.
  6. Add spinach and lemon juice, cover until wilted (~30 sec).
  7. Pour in cream, broth, seasoning, pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Simmer 2 min.
  8. Add butter and stir to melt and mix.
  9. Stir in pasta and pasta water. Simmer 1-2 min until pasta is fully cooked.
  10. Turn off heat, add grated pecorino, and mix well.
  11. Finish with a squeeze of lemon and extra cheese if desired. Serve hot.
