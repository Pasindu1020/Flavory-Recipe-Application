Bread Pizza

Bread Pizza is the perfect quick-fix snack that combines the cheesy satisfaction of pizza with the simplicity of toasted bread. Itâ€™s a fun and flavorful alternative to traditional pizza, using everyday pantry ingredients like sandwich bread, veggies, pizza sauce, and melted cheese. Whether you're hosting a party, craving a midnight snack, or looking for a fun way to get kids involved in the kitchen, bread pizza is a versatile go-to recipe thatâ€™s ready in minutes and bursting with flavor.

Prep Time

15 mins

Cook Time

30 mins

Total Time

45 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 or 5 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp oregano (if available)

White Sauce

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp bread flour
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 tsp oregano (if available)

Pizza Sauce

  • 3 tbsp tomato sauce or ketchup
  • 1 clove of garlic (chopped)
  • 1/2 tbsp chili flakes
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp oregano (if available)

Directions

  1. Take 4 large slices or 5 small slices of bread and finely chop them into small pieces.
  2. In a mixing bowl, crack 2 eggs and add 1/2 cup of milk. Whisk until well combined.
  3. Add 1 tsp oregano, 1/2 tsp chili flakes, 1/2 tsp pepper, and 1/2 tsp salt to the egg mixture.
  4. Mix well to blend all the spices evenly.
  5. Add the chopped bread into the mixture.
  6. Gently fold everything together until the bread is well soaked and coated.
  7. Let it sit for a minute to absorb the liquid before cooking.

White Sauce Recipe

  1. Add 2 tablespoons of butter to a pan and melt over low heat.
  2. Once melted, stir in 1 teaspoon of wheat flour and mix well to form a roux.
  3. Gradually add 1/2 cup of milk while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Add oregano if available, and let the sauce simmer for a few minutes until slightly thickened. Set aside to cool.
  5. In a bowl, combine 3 tablespoons of tomato sauce or ketchup with 1 tablespoon of chili flakes, a little oregano, 3 chopped garlic cloves, 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder, and a pinch of salt.
  6. Mix well until it forms a flavorful sauce to enhance your white sauce base.

Making the Pizza

  1. First, add some oil or butter to a pan and heat it.
  2. Add the bread mixture to the pan and spread it evenly.
  3. Close the lid and cook over low flame.
  4. After 1 minute, when one side is cooked, flip it using a plate.
  5. Apply some more oil or butter to the pan.
  6. Add the bread mixture again (if making in layers or more than one batch).
  7. Spread a layer of pizza sauce on top.
  8. Add a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  9. 10 minutes before using the mozzarella cheese, put it in a diffuser and grate it â€“ this helps it melt properly.
  10. If you don't have cheese, you can use the white sauce instead.
  11. Top with any pizza toppings you like (e.g., vegetables, meats).
  12. Add another layer of mozzarella cheese.
  13. Add onion rings on top.
  14. Close the lid and cook for 1 more minute.
  15. Remove the pizza carefully and serve hot.
