Sweet Potato Mini Pancakes

These Sweet Potato Mini Pancakes are the perfect blend of cozy comfort and healthy goodness! Made with creamy mashed sweet potatoes, these golden bites are soft, fluffy, and naturally sweet with hints of cinnamon and vanilla. They're a wonderful way to sneak in extra nutrients, especially for kids, and make a great option for breakfast, brunch, or even a midday snack. Whether topped with maple syrup, a sprinkle of powdered sugar, or a spoonful of yogurt and berries, each bite is warm, satisfying, and packed with flavor. Prep Time

15 mins

Cook Time

30 mins

Total Time

45 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 medium sweet potato (about 200g)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Directions

  1. Prepare the Sweet Potato:
  2. Peel and cut the sweet potato into chunks.
  3. Steam or boil until tender, about 10â€“15 minutes.
  4. Mash until smooth and let it cool slightly.

Make the Batter

  1. In a bowl, combine the mashed sweet potato, egg, and sugar.
  2. Mix in the flour, baking powder, cinnamon (if using), and salt until well combined.

Cook the Pancakes

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil.
  2. Spoon small amounts of batter onto the skillet to form mini pancakes.
  3. Cook for 2â€“3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.

Serve

Serve warm with your choice of toppings, such as honey, maple syrup, or fresh fruit.
