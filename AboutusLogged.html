Pasindu Harshamal

Pasindu’s journey into the culinary world started with a simple love for food and a big appetite. What began as helping his grandmother prepare meals turned into a lifelong passion for creating delicious and hearty dishes. He believes that good food should tell a story and bring people closer, which is why he specializes in soulful, home-cooked meals. Whether it’s a slow-cooked stew or a sizzling grill, he puts his heart into every plate he serves.

Pramudi Premarathna

Pramudi’s kitchen is her creative playground, where she transforms everyday ingredients into works of art. Inspired by her mother’s traditional recipes, she has mastered the balance between heritage and innovation. She believes that food is a form of self-expression, and every dish she creates tells a story. From vibrant salads to aromatic curries, her creations are a reflection of her love for fresh, flavorful, and wholesome cooking.

Isheni Thamel

Isheni has always been fascinated by the art of baking. From delicate pastries to indulgent chocolate cakes, she finds joy in perfecting the smallest details that turn a good dessert into an unforgettable experience. Her love for the kitchen began when she was gifted her first recipe book as a child, and she hasn’t stopped experimenting since. Today, she’s known for her ability to transform simple ingredients into sweet masterpieces that bring smiles to everyone’s faces.

Vimesh Gunasekaran

For Vimesh, cooking is more than just a skill—it’s an adventure. His passion for bold flavors and global cuisines has taken him on a journey of discovery, learning from different cultures and incorporating diverse techniques into his cooking. Whether it’s fiery street food from Asia or a rustic European dish, he loves blending ingredients to create something unique. His motto? “A good meal should always leave you wanting just one more bite.”

Lourdes Shenuka

From a young age, Shenuka found joy in experimenting with flavors, often surprising her family with creative dishes. Growing up in a home where food was a way of bringing people together, she quickly developed a deep passion for cooking. Her signature dishes blend traditional flavors with a modern twist, making every meal both comforting and exciting. When she’s not in the kitchen, you’ll find her exploring local markets in search of fresh ingredients and new inspirations.

How It All Started

Five passionate chefs, one shared dream—Flavory was born from a love for food and the desire to share it with the world. Shenuka's creativity, Pasindu’s comforting flavors, Isheni’s mastery of baking, Vimesh’s global inspirations, and Pramudi’s eye for innovation came together to build a space where anyone could find joy in cooking. What started as kitchen experiments and late-night food talks turned into a website filled with recipes that bring people together. Flavory isn’t just about food—it’s about stories, flavors, and the magic of cooking made accessible to all.
